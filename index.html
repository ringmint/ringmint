About Ring Mint

A modern concierge backed by decades of jewelry experience.

Ring Mint was created by Chloe Alpert with the support of her family’s jewelry business, Teaman Jewelry, which has been in business since 1974.

The model is intentionally different: no showroom, no inventory, and no reason to steer you toward a stone or setting that is not right for you. We source globally, design from scratch, and make each ring only after it is approved.

The result is a more personal process, better pricing, and a ring that feels like it could only belong to one person.
